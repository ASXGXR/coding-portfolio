Poliiglot Languages App

Poliiglot is an AI-driven language learning app designed to offer a personalized and interactive learning experience. The project began with a vision to create an intuitive and engaging platform for users eager to explore new languages. My role encompassed UI/UX design, branding, and conceptualizing AI features, all crafted meticulously in Figma for iOS.

User Interface and Experience

The design process started with extensive user research to understand the needs and preferences of language learners. With these insights, I crafted a clean, intuitive interface in Figma, focusing on simplicity and ease of navigation. The color scheme and typography were chosen to enhance readability and user engagement.

Features and Functionality

Poliiglot integrates advanced AI algorithms to adapt to individual learning styles, offering customized quizzes, pronunciation practice, and interactive dialogues. I focused on designing features that foster a gamified learning experience, such as progress tracking and rewards, to keep users motivated and engaged.

Branding and Visual Identity

The Poliiglot brand identity reflects the fusion of technology and language. The logo and app icon were designed to be memorable and symbolic of global communication, using vibrant colors and fluid shapes to convey connectivity and diversity.

Final Thoughts

Developing Poliiglot was a journey that combined my passion for design with the transformative power of AI in education. This project allowed me to explore the intricacies of app design and the potential of AI to create personalized learning experiences. Poliiglot stands as a testament to the role of design in shaping how we learn and interact with new languages.
