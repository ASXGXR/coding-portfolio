Skull & Bones Rum

I was tasked with creating a rum brand from scratch. This endeavor was an exciting journey through branding, product design, and digital presence. It provided me with valuable insights into 3D design, branding, and photo editing.

Concept and Visual Identity

Guided by the research findings, the concept of Skull & Bones was born, symbolizing adventure, mystery, and the spirit of exploration. I crafted the logo using Adobe Illustrator. Many variations were produced to ensure a clear and precise pirate rum essence. See below to visualise how I applied the logo to the final piece.

Product Design in Blender

The heart of this project was the creation of the rum bottle, meticulously modeled in Blender. Attention was paid to every detail, from the shape that echoes the brand's adventurous spirit to the intricate cap design with thumb grip. The 3D modeling process ensured a realistic and compelling presentation of the product.

Web Design and Development

Bringing Skull & Bones Rum to the digital world, I crafted a website to serve as a selling point for the rum brand.

Final Thoughts

The Skull & Bones Rum project was a comprehensive exercise in brand creation, from conceptualization to final product and digital presence. Each step of the journey was an opportunity to blend creativity with functionality, resulting in a brand that stands out in the crowded spirits market.
