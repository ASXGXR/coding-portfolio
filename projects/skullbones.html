Skull & Bones Rum

I was tasked with creating a rum brand from scratch. This endeavor was an exciting journey through branding, product design, and digital presence. It provided me with valuable insights into 3D design, website creation, and photo editing.

Product Design in Blender

The heart of this project was the creation of the rum bottle, meticulously modeled in Blender. Attention was paid to minor details, from the barrel shape that echoes the brand's adventurous spirit to the intricate cap design with thumb grip. The 3D modeling process ensured a realistic and compelling presentation of the product. I further utilised this design as a base to create other flavours and prototypes.

Concept and Visual Identity

Guided by the research findings, the concept of Skull & Bones was born, symbolizing adventure and mystery. The logo adheres to Gestalt principles, ensuring unity and recognizability. Serifs in the typography mirror the crossbones, subtly emphasizing the brand theme, while kerning ensures versatility and readability.

Web Design and Development

Bringing Skull & Bones Rum to the digital world, I crafted a website in Wordpress to serve as a selling point for the rum brand.

Final Thoughts

Creating the Skull & Bones Rum brand was an exciting journey of 3D modelling, branding and discovery. I gained a greater knowledge of 3D rendering, texturing and lighting through courses and intuition. I applied design principles to the packaging and logo, while keeping it fresh and unique. Skull&Bones provided me with valuable insights into what it takes to design a spirits brand from the ground up, and I will continue to grow and build on this project in the future.
